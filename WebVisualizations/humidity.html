Humidity

There is no correlation between latitude and humidity. There are a few clusters that a very faint. One is around 60 degrees latitude and 80% humidity. The other is around -10 degrees and just below 100% humidity. There are also a few outliers between 60 and 80 degrees latitude that has 0% humidity, which is a bit suspect.
